Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Bear ("we", "us", "our") provides custom personal website design services. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit liltayara.github.io/Bearsite or place an order with us, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Google Sign-In: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo via Firebase Authentication. This is used only to identify you and pre-fill your order form.
  • Order details: When you submit an order, we collect the information you provide — such as your name, email, and project requirements — and process it to prepare and deliver your website.
  • Payment information: Payments are processed directly by PayPal. We never see or store your card or bank details.
  • Basic technical data: Standard, non-identifying data such as browser type may be collected automatically for security and performance purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your order
  • To communicate with you about your project
  • To improve our website and services
  • To prevent fraud, spam, and abuse

We do not sell your personal information to third parties, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. How We Share Information

We only share information with the services required to operate Bear:

  • Google / Firebase — for authentication
  • Telegram — order details are relayed to our order-management bot so we can process your request
  • PayPal — for payment processing

These providers process data under their own privacy policies. We do not share your data with any other third party for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain order and account information only for as long as needed to deliver your project, respond to support requests, and meet legal or accounting obligations. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 6).

5. Security

We use industry-standard practices — including HTTPS encryption and Firebase's authentication infrastructure — to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you at any time by contacting us (see below). We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Cookies & Local Storage

We use minimal browser storage strictly to keep you signed in and to remember your order progress. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.

8. Children's Privacy

Bear's services are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
